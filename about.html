Bloated web

People have to process and filter immense quantities of data for essential information daily. The good news, most of the information is available in the world wide web. The bad news, the information is buried under a heap of useless data. The web is bloated. Websites are full of distracting advertising, animations, style sheets and javascript code making it hard and exhausting to find information.

The web should be available for everyone and serve all of humanity.

How pyour web can help people ?

pyour web (pyour pronounced as in pure (/pj~Jr/))) brings the web back to the people by decluttering the websites and reducing it to the essentials, to words.
